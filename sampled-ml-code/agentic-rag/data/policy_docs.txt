# Refund Policy

## Overview
We strive to provide excellent service. Our refund policy ensures fair treatment for all customers.

## Eligibility
- Refunds are available for orders delayed by more than 7 days
- Products must be in original condition
- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of delivery

## Process
1. Contact customer support with your order ID
2. Provide reason for refund request
3. Our team will review within 2 business days
4. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

## Exceptions
- Refunds over $500 require manager approval
- Digital products are not eligible for refunds
- Custom orders have limited refund options

## Contact
For refund requests, please create a support ticket or call our hotline.
